Height & Weight

A human's height and build vary from ethnic group to ethnic group but the average is marked around 1.5m (5ft) to 1.8m (6ft) and 50kg (110lb) to 150kg (330lb).

Speed

The base walking speed from an average human 30ft.

Versatile

Humans are very versatile in their nature, be able to adapt as they need. You gain one feat of your choice and one skill or language of your choice.

Reeric

Native to the eastern island of C____ Holding, the Reeric Humans are generally tall and muscular, with light dusky skin that tends to a darker colour the farther south they dwell. Most have raven-black hair, but those who live farther north have red or brown hair. The most common eye colour for a Reeric is green but other lighter colours do exist.
