Fyre Isles Archive

Welcome to the Fyre Isles Archive, a archive about the Fyre Isles campaign setting for the 5e of Dungeons & Dragons. This archive was created with the intention of creating a complete and readily available repository of official information about the Fyre Isles setting developed by Owen McGregor in 2017.

Please be warned that some of the content in this archive will contain spoilers about the Fyre Isles setting. We will recommend not exploring this archive if you are a player in a Fyre Isles campagin.

The Fyre Isles was written by Owen McGregor
